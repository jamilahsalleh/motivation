My motivation factors towards online education:

1. REALISTIC GOALS

There are two types of goals:
1. Learning goals
2. Performance goals

Learning goals is a goal which tends me to focus on learning for understanding, developing new skills and improving or developing competence where the standard for judging the achievement.
Performance goals is a goal which tends me to focus on demonstrating competence or ability where the standard for measurement is in comparison to others.

2. INTEREST

There are two types of interest:
1. Individual interest
2. Situational interest

Individual interest is seen as a relatively stable disposition or motivational orientation towards certain activities.
Situational interest is engendered in response to particular conditions within the environment and tends to be less enduring.
